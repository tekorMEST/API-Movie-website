lastest release

There might be fewer movies in 2024 compared to 2023 because of last year's writers' and actors' strikes some studios even pushed titles because of that — but there is certainly no shortage of entertainment heading to theaters or available on streaming services.

The year is perhaps defined by the many sequels on the release calendar: Denis Villeneuve's Dune: Part Two, starring Timothée Chalamet, Zendaya, Austin Butler, and Florence Pugh; Ryan Reynolds and Hugh Jackman in Deadpool & Wolverine; Jack Black is back for Kung Fu Panda 4; Paul Rudd & Co. return for Ghostbusters: Frozen Empire; plus Zack Snyder's Rebel Moon — Part Two: The Scargiver, Venom 3, Gladiator 2, Inside Out 2, Despicable Me 4, and Joaquin Phoenix and Lady Gaga in Joker: Folie à Deux.

Other hotly anticipated titles in the Mad Max spinoff/prequel Furiosa, starring Anya Taylor-Joy and Chris Hemsworth, the Mean Girls movie musical, Ryan Gosling and Emily Blunt in The Fall Guy, the Lion King spinoff Mufasa, and the first of two Wicked movies starring Cynthia Erivo and Ariana Grande. Mark your calendars, and be sure to check back as we update this article throughout the year.
